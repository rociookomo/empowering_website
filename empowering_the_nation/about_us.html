About Us

Welcome to Empowering the Nation, where we are dedicated to enhancing domestic workers' and gardeners' skills and opportunities. Our commitment, founded in 2018, is inspired by a deep-rooted desire to provide the same opportunities for growth and education that were unavailable to our founderâ€™s family and community.

Based in Johannesburg, we offer comprehensive training programs to make our participants more marketable and competitive in the job market. Our six-month courses include first aid, sewing, landscaping, and life skills, while our six-week short courses cover child minding, cooking, and garden maintenance.

At Empowering the Nation, we believe in empowering individuals to seek better employment opportunities and start their businesses. We offer a range of courses with discounts for multiple enrollments, ensuring that our services are accessible and affordable.

Join us in transforming lives and communities through the transformative power of skill development and education. Together, we can inspire change and create a brighter future for all.
